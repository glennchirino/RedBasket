How do I purchase RedBasket offers?
To purchase a RedBasket offer, select the offer and choose the "Order" button. Follow the checkout prompts to complete your purchase.

How do I pay for RedBasket offers?
Pay for your RedBasket offers with your PayPal account.

How do I redeem RedBasket offers?
To redeem your RedBasket offer, just show the receipt on your phone when redeeming. To display a previously purchased offer, just select "Order History" to view past purchases.
